Grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato

INGREDIENT

  • slices cooked bacon
  • tablespoons soft butter, divided (or to taste)
  • slices bread
  • slices monterey jack pepper cheese (or use American cheese)
  • slices tomatoes

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1- 2 slices of tomato and 2 slices cooked bacon, then 1 more slice of cheese.
Step 2 - Cover with a slice of bread (butter-side facing outside).
Step 3 -Fry over medium turning once with a spatula until golden brown on both sides.
